About Our School

Army Public School has been a pioneer in providing quality education to the children of defense personnel and civilians. With a sprawling campus and state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure that every student gets the best environment to grow.


Our focus is not just on academics but on character building, sportsmanship, and discipline.
